Business Requirement Document (BRD) for Simple CRM Application
1. Introduction
Purpose: Define requirements for a simple CRM system to manage customer data, interactions, and sales pipeline.
Scope: Web-based application with role-based access for Admin, Sales, and Support teams.
Assumptions:
* - Application will integrate with email and export data to Excel.
* - GDPR compliance for data privacy.
2. Functional Modules & Screens
A. Login & User Management
Functionalities:
* Secure login with username/password.
* Role-based dashboard (Admin, Sales, Support).
* Password reset via email.
Detailed Requirements:
* Username must exist in the system.
* Password complexity: Minimum 8 characters, at least 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special character.
* Account lock after 5 failed attempts.
* Session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity.
* Password reset token expires in 30 minutes.
B. Dashboard
Functionalities:
* Overview of leads, opportunities, and tasks.
* Quick links to add customer or lead.
Detailed Requirements:
* Auto-refresh every 5 minutes.
* Display KPIs based on user role.
* Drill-down links to respective lists.
C. Customer Management
Functionalities:
* Customer List: Search, filter, sort.
* Customer Details: View and edit profile.
Detailed Requirements:
* Mandatory fields: Name, Email, Phone.
* Email validation: Regex ^[\w-\.]+@([\w-]+\.)+[\w-]{2,4}$.
* Phone validation: Numeric, 10 digits.
* Prevent duplicates based on Email.
* Status options: Active, Inactive.
* Audit trail for changes (who, when).
D. Lead Management
Functionalities:
* Lead List: Filter by status (New, In Progress, Closed).
* Lead Details: Assign to sales rep, update status.
Detailed Requirements:
* Status workflow: New ? In Progress ? Closed.
* Mandatory fields: Lead Name, Source, Contact Info.
* Assigned user must exist in system.
* Source options: Website, Referral, Campaign.
* Validate no duplicate leads for same customer.
E. Opportunity Management
Functionalities:
* Opportunity List: Filter by stage (Prospect, Proposal, Won, Lost).
* Opportunity Details: Link to customer and lead.
Detailed Requirements:
* Stage workflow: Prospect ? Proposal ? Won/Lost.
* Probability auto-calculated: Prospect: 20%, Proposal: 50%, Won: 100%, Lost: 0%.
* Amount must be numeric and > 0.
* Linked Customer must exist in Customer table.
F. Task Management
Functionalities:
* Task List: View tasks by due date.
* Task Details: Assign, set priority.
Detailed Requirements:
* Due date cannot be in the past.
* Priority options: High, Medium, Low.
* Assigned user must exist in system.
* Status options: Pending, Completed.
G. Reports
Functionalities:
* Export customer and lead data to Excel.
* Generate monthly sales report.
Detailed Requirements:
* Admin can export all data.
* Sales users can export only their assigned leads.
* Reports must include filters (date range, status).
3. Non-Functional Requirements
* - Performance: Page load < 3 seconds.
* - Security: SSL, role-based access.
* - Compliance: GDPR for customer data.
* - Scalability: Support up to 10,000 records.
4. Integrations
* - Email notifications for lead assignment.
* - API for external data sync (REST-based).
